About Me

Hello, I am Gourav Singh Chauhan. I am passionate about leveraging industrial engineering principles and data-driven insights to optimize processes and drive operational excellence. I have a strong command of data visualization tools like Tableau and Minitab, and possess expertise in AutoCAD, Python, ERP, MS Excel, SolidWorks. I am skilled in supply chain methodologies including Lean Six Sigma, linear programming, root cause analysis, and ABC segmentation.

I have more than 3 years of hands-on experience where I managed the ERP system, defined KPIs, and conducted data analysis. I also worked as an Operation Executive, overseeing daily operations and implementing quality control measures. My experience in both manufacturing and logistics has deepened my understanding of process optimization.
